Isometric Food

Nowadays kids, and people in general, spent a lot of time playing video games on their parents phones, or on their own phones, this can be counterproductive as people are not taking advantage of their free time to learn something new, or to improve their abilities to be better prepare for a technological world, a world where a lot of problems we don't even know yet will arise. The future is bright but we will need people with a logical mindset to solve the problems the technology will embrace, because of this we want people to start developing and training the skills needed such as logical thinking and memory by playing a simple game as Isometric Food is. Players will also see concepts of programming such as main functions and loops.
